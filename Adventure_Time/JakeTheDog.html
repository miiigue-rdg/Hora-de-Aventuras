Jake the dog

The elastic warrior

In a world where the impossible is ordinary and magic flows through the corners of time, there exists a one-of-a-kind creature: a dog with powers that defy logic and a heart as big as his fists. His name is Jake, and he’s much more than a pet — he’s the brother, mentor, shield, and soul mate of Finn the Human. Blessed with magical abilities that allow him to stretch, shrink, shapeshift, and transform into almost anything, Jake is a lazy yet powerful warrior, a free-spirited musician, and an unexpectedly wise guide. He may love naps and snacks more than battles, but when his family or kingdom is in danger, Jake becomes an unstoppable force. From the fields of the Candy Kingdom to the darkest corners of the Land of Ooo, Jake the Dog has left his mark with laid-back humor, unwavering loyalty, and a unique way of seeing the world. Because sometimes, true heroes don’t wear armor... they carry a guitar and pancakes!
