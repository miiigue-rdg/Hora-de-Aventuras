The Lich

The magician of death

The Lich is the embodiment of pure evil and destruction in the world of Adventure Time.

As a powerful undead sorcerer, he relentlessly seeks to bring chaos and annihilation to the Land of Ooo.

His eerie presence and dark magic make him one of Finn and Jake's most formidable foes.

Despite his terrifying nature, his story is intertwined with the deeper lore and mysteries of the series.
