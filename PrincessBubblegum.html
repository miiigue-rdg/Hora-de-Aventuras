Princess Bubblegum

The pink queen

In the brightest corner of the Candy Kingdom, where sweets walk and dreams taste like cotton candy, rules a mind as sharp as it is sweet: Princess Bubblegum. Also known as Bonnibel Bubblegum, she is far more than a royal figure. She’s a scientist, an inventor, a strategist… and an unstoppable force when her people or justice are at stake. Her sugary smile hides a razor-sharp intellect. She has created life from sugar, built impossible machines, and faced world-ending threats with cool logic and a pink grin. She may look charming, but inside burns the fire of a ruler who would do anything to protect what she loves. From leading candy armies to bending the laws of time and matter, Princess Bubblegum is the perfect fusion of adorable and formidable. Because in the Land of Ooo, true royalty doesn’t just wear a crown… she wears a lab coat too.
